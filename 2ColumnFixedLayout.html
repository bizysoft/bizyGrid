This is a fixed layout with a fixed header and footer height.

Column's will remain horizontally centered until your browser window is too small. Vertically, content will push the footer if your browser window becomes too small. Scrollbars are used when required.

Try it by resizing your browser window.

Fixed width layout may suit better if you have a combination of text and images because they will remain in proportion to each other, not your browser window. In this layout, the content will stay a constant horizontal size.

Notice how the footer stays at the bottom when the columns are larger than the content.

You will also notice that the column's stretch from header to footer.
