This is a fluid layout with a fixed header and footer height.

This cell will constrain the content horizontally and push the footer when space is required. Vertical scrollbars are used when required.

A single CSS entry was changed from the 'Fixed' demo to achieve this layout.

Fluid layouts suit better when you don't want your content to be lopped or scrolled horizontally. They are best for mobile device browsers. In this layout, content cell's will remain the same relative horizontal size to your browser window.

Notice how the footer stays at the bottom when the cell is larger than the content.

Try it by resizing your browser window.
